Our Story

History of City Citadel of Grace Family

Founded 2021

City Citadel of Grace Family was founded in 2021 by Hon. Arc. Min. Christopher Joshua James JP with a clear divine mandate to raise a people rooted in grace, truth, and kingdom purpose. What began as a small fellowship of passionate believers has, over the past five years, grown into a vibrant spiritual family committed to transforming lives and communities through the power of God's Word.

From its inception, the ministry has emphasized sound teaching, fervent worship, and intentional discipleship, creating an atmosphere where individuals are equipped to discover their identity and function effectively in God's kingdom. Through consistent gatherings, outreach programs, and leadership development initiatives, City Citadel of Grace Family has steadily expanded its reach and impact.

Today, the ministry stands as a beacon of hope and empowerment, nurturing men, women, and youth to live purpose-driven lives while advancing the gospel both locally and beyond.

"Its journey over the past five years reflects a testimony of growth, resilience, and unwavering commitment to its founding vision: building lives, restoring destinies, and establishing God's kingdom on earth."
