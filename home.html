Who We Are

A Family Built on Grace

City Citadel of Grace Family is a non-denominational fellowship rooted in the love of God and the power of community. We are a family of believers committed to winning souls, growing in faith, and impacting our city for Christ. Whether you are visiting for the first time or looking for a place to belong — you are welcome here.
